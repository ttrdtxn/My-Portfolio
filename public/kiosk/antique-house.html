Antique House×

The Antique House’s architectural design reflects Spanish colonial influence. The building is a prewar vintage structure and considered to be one of the residences of Carriedo family. It served as a garrison of the Japanese forces during the Second World War. It was renovated and restored on January 5, 1979. The building is made of concrete slab, concrete columns supporting the second floor wooden frames and roofing tiles supported by wooden trusses.
