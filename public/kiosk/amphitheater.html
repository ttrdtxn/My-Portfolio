Amphitheater×

A multi-purpose open-air structure within the campus. It serves as a venue for various events and activities, contributing to the cultural and social vibrancy of the university.

The amphitheater is a popular venue for student organization meetings, general assemblies, and university-wide gatherings, fostering collaboration and community-building among students and staff.

When not in use for formal events, the amphitheater serves as a leisure space where students and visitors can relax, socialize, or simply enjoy the natural surroundings of the Lagoon Park.
